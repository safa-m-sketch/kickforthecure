What is Kick for the Cure?

A special martial arts-based event promoting cardiovascular health among children while simultaneously raising funds for the American Red Cross and its lifesaving blood donation efforts. Kids will take part in a fun, beginner-friendly martial arts event focused on safe techniques, movement, and learning how exercise keeps both the heart and body strong. The event also features a brief educational presentation on anatomy, fitness, and how healthy habits promote lifelong wellness. By combining movement, education, and compassion, Kick for the Cure empowers kids to learn about their health while giving back to their community.
